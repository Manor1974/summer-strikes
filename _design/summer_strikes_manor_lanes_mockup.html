Is shoe rental included? +

Standard shoe rental rates apply and are not included in the Summer Strikes program.

How do I get my daily vouchers? +

Vouchers reset each morning and are available in your member dashboard. You'll also receive an optional text alert via Twilio if you opted in during registration.

Can I add more children later? +

Yes — log into your account, go to My Children, and add a new child. They must be ages 2–15 and live in your household.

Can this be used for daycare or camp groups? +

No. This program is not valid for daycare outings, camps, business outings, or birthday party events.

What is the Family Pass? +

The optional Family Pass lets adults bowl alongside their children during Summer Strikes hours for a one-time seasonal fee.
